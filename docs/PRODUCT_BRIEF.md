# Product Brief — Auri App-first Family OS

## Thesis

Auri should launch as an app-first family AI operating layer. Auri Robot comes later as the premium physical endpoint that unlocks real-world sessions.

## Product definition

Auri App helps parents turn scattered family context into organized work objects:

- Calendar prep
- Photo and video moments
- Child learning journeys
- Baby care logging
- Meal prep flows
- Family sharing drafts
- House rules and permissions

Auri Robot later adds:

- Reading with Auri
- Piano/practice session capture
- Homework/object input
- Family cameraman clips
- Real-world context capture from the living room

## Core insight

Most family software only understands what people type. Auri understands family life through calendar, photos, messages, app connections, and eventually real-world sessions through Auri Robot.

## Demo positioning

The demo should make viewers believe:

1. This app is useful without the robot.
2. The robot meaningfully unlocks more contexts later.
3. Auri is not a camera app; it is a family AI OS.
4. The UI is not chat-first. It is work-object-first.

## Key product objects

- NeedItem: something waiting for parent decision.
- Suggestion: an action the parent can take or delegate to a helper.
- CalendarEvent: plan or AI suggestion with preparation state.
- Journey: an ongoing help stream for a child or family.
- Moment: unified media/event timeline item from Auri, Phone, Reading, or external sources.
- FamilyMember: person profile and permissions.
- Connection: data/app source, such as Apple Calendar, iCloud Photos, Auri Robot, Doubao.
- HouseRule: family-level behavior/permission rule.

## Agent naming

UI should use names sparingly, as helper signatures, not as top-level module names:

- Milo: skill / practice / lesson helper.
- Bibi: reading helper.
- Mira: moments / photo / video helper.
- Nora: home coordination / calendar / school notes / meal prep.

Do not use labels like `Memory Agent`, `Calendar Agent`, or `Camera Agent`.

## Voice

Warm, premium, understated.

Good:
- `Piano plan is ready.`
- `Mira found 2 clips Grandma may love.`
- `Nora found a form waiting for your decision.`

Avoid:
- `AI generated insights are available.`
- `Analyze your family data.`
- `Ask anything.`
